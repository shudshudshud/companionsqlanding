Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. We never import your contacts or sync with social media. Your reflections are processed securely and never used to train models. See our full privacy policy for details.

How does the text analysis work?

You write natural reflections about your day with friends. Our system identifies patterns, shared interests, and relationship insights while you always review and confirm what was found.

What if I want to stop using CompanionSquared?

You can export all your data or delete your account anytime. We also provide the source code so you can run your own version if needed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Do I need to use it every day?

Daily reflection works best, but even weekly check-ins provide valuable friendship insights. Start with whatever feels sustainable.
