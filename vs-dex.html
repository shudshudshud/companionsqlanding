CompanionSquared vs Dex

Both help you maintain relationships, but serve completely different purposes. Here's how to choose.

The Core Difference

CompanionSquared

Friendship Reflection & Connection Tool

CompanionSquared is designed to help you become a more thoughtful friend through daily reflection, while also creating opportunities for meaningful connections. It uses AI to help you understand your friendship patterns, remember shared interests, and naturally discover when to bring people together for activities they'd both enjoy.

Dex

Professional Networking CRM

Dex is built to help you maintain professional relationships and leverage your network for career advancement. It syncs with LinkedIn, tracks job changes, and reminds you to stay "warm" with professional contacts. The focus is on turning acquaintances into allies and expanding your professional network.

Who Each Tool Is Really For

Understanding your primary goal helps you choose the right tool

CompanionSquared Users

  • The Thoughtful Friend
    Wants to remember important details about close friends and be more present in relationships
  • The Busy Professional
    Career-focused but wants to maintain genuine friendships outside of work
  • The Reflective Journaler
    Already journals and wants to focus specifically on friendship growth
  • The Mindful Connector
    Prefers deep, meaningful relationships over large social networks

Dex Users

  • The Active Networker
    Regularly meets new people through work events and wants to maintain those connections
  • The Career Climber
    Understands that professional relationships are key to career advancement
  • The Freelancer/Entrepreneur
    Needs to maintain relationships with clients, partners, and potential collaborators
  • The LinkedIn Power User
    Already active on professional social networks and wants better organization

Different Tools, Different Friendship Philosophies

CompanionSquared Approach:

Your companions' companion believes that better friendships come from mindful reflection and creating opportunities for meaningful connection. It's about deepening existing relationships through thoughtful attention, while also discovering natural ways to bring friends together around shared interests and activities.

Dex Approach:

Dex believes in the power of maintaining a warm professional network and turning acquaintances into allies. It's excellent for people who understand that career success often depends on who you know and want to systematically nurture those professional relationships.

The heart of it: Are you looking to become a more thoughtful friend, or a more strategic networker? Both are valuable—they're just different paths.
